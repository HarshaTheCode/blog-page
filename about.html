My Story

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm Harsha, a passionate college student and solo developer based in Hyderabad, India. What started as curiosity about how websites work has evolved into a deep passion for creating digital experiences that make a difference.

As a self-taught developer, I've spent countless hours learning, building, and breaking things (and then fixing them again!). Every project is a new adventure, every bug is a puzzle waiting to be solved, and every line of code is an opportunity to create something meaningful.

This blog is where I share my journey through the ever-evolving world of technology. From web development insights to the latest tech breakthroughs, I write about things that fascinate me and hopefully inspire others who are on a similar path.
